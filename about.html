I am a 5th year statistics PhD student at the University of Arizona minoring in Agricultural Resource Economics. My research involves accounting for endogeneity when quantifying the role of risk in agricultural technology adoption decisions. I simultaneously model yield and improved seed use, a binary variable and use the lagged yield and lagged yield variance to quantify risk. A maximum likelihood and bayesian approach is used.

Research Areas

  • Correlated binary and continous outcomes
  • Longitudinal data
  • Bayesian Statistics
  • Endogeneity
